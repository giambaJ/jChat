jChat is an overlay that allows you to show your Twitch chat on screen with OBS, XSplit, and any other streaming software that supports browser sources. It supports your BetterTTV, FrankerFaceZ and 7TV emotes, always at the best available quality. You can choose to activate a smooth animation, show bots messages and fade old ones after some time. It comes with many fonts and styling options that can be combined as desired.
